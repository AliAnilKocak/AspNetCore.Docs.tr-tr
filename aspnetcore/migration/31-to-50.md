## <a name="update-the-target-framework"></a><span data-ttu-id="a27fc-114">Hedef çerçeveyi güncelleştirme</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a27fc-114">Update the target framework</span></span>

```diff

  <PropertyGroup>
-    <TargetFramework>netcoreapp3.1</TargetFramework>
+    <TargetFramework>netcoreapp5.0</TargetFramework>
  </PropertyGroup>

</Project>
```
